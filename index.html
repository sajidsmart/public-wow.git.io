Welcome to Women of Mystery (WoM). We are the first web3 storytelling collective to bridge stories across women-led and non-biary NFT worlds. We welcome all storytellers interested in building ideas into comic books, screenplays, stage scripts,and novels. This community aims to support writers making their way from script to screen and changing the world together - one word at a time.

DID YOU KNOW THAT...

In 2021, men appeared on screen 2x as many times as women

Only 11 of the highest grossing 100 films in 2021 featured a woman of color as lead or co-lead

27% percent of all DC and Marvel characters are female, and only 12% of mainstream superhero comics have female protagonists. Behind the scenes, 39% of comic book writers are women, and only 20% are non-white.

And thats exactly why weve made it our mission to create an inclusive ecosystem that is open to any creator no matter where you come from, what you look like or who you know.

A JUMPCUT PROJECT

We are a web3 entertainment company that is democratizing Hollywood through harnessing our collective creativity to build IP and premium storytelling brands from the bottom up.

Meet the first member of our collective and star of the show: Eva Morales. Eva is a problem solver, truth seeker, certifiable badass and World of Womens first private investigator. She is our window into the lives and experiences of women of all colors, beliefs, professions, passions, cultures, shapes and sizes. Together with her trustworthy and oh-so-smart companion, Evas mission in life is to tell the stories of smart, passionate, complicated, vulnerable and audacious women.

Women of Mystery strives to elevate female, non-binary and other underrepresented writ ers and creators, supporting them in the creation of diverse characters (like Eva!), stories and premium IP via Jumpcuts collaborative communities storytelling platform. Our first goal? Create a comic book that is avant-garde - created collectively by and for the community

  • Eva signs online. She is the first character to seed the WoM storytelling collective.1

Q3

2022

  • 750 Writers Room tickets are available to mint in September

  • Tickets unlock access to Jumpcuts proprietary members-only storytelling platform where creators and holders come together to collaborate on community-driven IP

Q4

2022

  • The Inaugural Writers Collective launches

  • Over the course of several months, ticket holders will give creative direction to an established writer - the story architect harnessing the communitys collective creativity to build out the story world, character arcs and key plot points - all inputs into the Women of Mystery comic book we will create together

  • Holders will participate in the storytelling by completing quests and earning points on the platform. Quests include voting on story direction, submitting loglines, pitches, character ideas, and more.

  • Holders will create alongside and learn from [story architect] and Winnie Kemp, gaining real-world experience of how to develop and pitch IP.

  • Our comic book goes into production; ticket holders will get an exclusive, limited edition physical collectible and other member perks.
